## What the ICP Is

The ICP defines the customer most likely to: (1) buy quickly, (2) use deeply, (3) expand over time, and (4) advocate. It is derived from your best existing customers — not from who you wish would buy from you.

**The ICP is not "everyone who could theoretically buy." It is the top 20% that generates 80% of value.**
