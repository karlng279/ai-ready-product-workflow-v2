# ST-005: Acceptance Criteria

**Story:** Select ECC for booking  
**USL Reference:** [../usl.md#st-005-select-ecc-for-booking](../usl.md#st-005-select-ecc-for-booking)  
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-13

---

## UI Elements

- **AC-001:** Eligible customers have a selectable ECC request option during booking.
- **AC-002:** The selection state is visibly shown after the customer selects ECC.

---

## UI Behavior

- **AC-003:** Selecting ECC attaches the service request to the current booking session.
- **AC-004:** Deselecting ECC removes the service request from the current booking session.
- **AC-005:** The customer can review service scope before or after selecting ECC.

---

## Logic

- **AC-006:** ECC can only be selected when the current booking remains eligible.
- **AC-007:** ECC selection captures booking ID, customer ID, selected time, content version, and price or request mode when available.

---

## Special Notes

- **AC-008:** If ECC selection fails, the customer sees a clear error and can continue booking without ECC.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **NFR-001:** ECC selection or deselection updates within 1 second for 95% of eligible booking sessions.
- **NFR-002:** ECC selection events are captured for 99% of successful selections.
