# ST-006: Acceptance Criteria

**Story:** Complete booking with or without ECC  
**USL Reference:** [../usl.md#st-006-complete-booking-with-or-without-ecc](../usl.md#st-006-complete-booking-with-or-without-ecc)  
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-13

---

## UI Elements

- **AC-001:** Booking completion remains available when ECC is not selected.
- **AC-002:** Booking completion shows ECC as selected when the customer chose the service.

---

## UI Behavior

- **AC-003:** The customer can complete booking after selecting ECC.
- **AC-004:** The customer can complete booking after declining or ignoring ECC.
- **AC-005:** If ECC becomes unavailable before completion, the customer can continue booking without ECC.

---

## Logic

- **AC-006:** Completed bookings preserve ECC selection status when selection is valid.
- **AC-007:** Booking abandonment after ECC exposure is measurable by exposed and non-exposed booking sessions.

---

## Special Notes

- **AC-008:** ECC must not become a required field for completing an export booking.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **NFR-001:** ECC offer handling must not increase booking completion page load time by more than 300 ms at P95.
- **NFR-002:** Booking completion status is recorded for 99% of sessions exposed to ECC.
