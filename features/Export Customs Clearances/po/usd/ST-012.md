# ST-012: Acceptance Criteria

**Story:** Track ECC performance metrics  
**USL Reference:** [../usl.md#st-012-track-ecc-performance-metrics](../usl.md#st-012-track-ecc-performance-metrics)  
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-13

---

## UI Elements

- **AC-001:** Product and operations users can view ECC performance metrics in a reporting surface or export.
- **AC-002:** Metrics include offer exposure, selection, booking completion, support case creation, first support action, support completion, and feedback.

---

## UI Behavior

- **AC-003:** Users can filter metrics by date range, lane, and customer segment when data is available.
- **AC-004:** When no matching data exists, the reporting surface shows an empty state instead of blank results.

---

## Logic

- **AC-005:** ECC selection rate is calculated as ECC selections divided by ECC offer views.
- **AC-006:** First support action SLA is calculated as selected ECC bookings with first action within 24 hours divided by selected ECC bookings.
- **AC-007:** Booking abandonment guardrail compares exposed booking sessions against a non-exposed or historical baseline.

---

## Special Notes

- **AC-008:** This story is marked Won't-have for the first delivery slice unless the team explicitly pulls analytics reporting into scope.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **NFR-001:** Metric calculations use event data captured for at least 99% of successful ECC selection events.
- **NFR-002:** Reports refresh within 24 hours for 95% of metric updates.
