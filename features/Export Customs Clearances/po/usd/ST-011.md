# ST-011: Acceptance Criteria

**Story:** Collect post-support feedback  
**USL Reference:** [../usl.md#st-011-collect-post-support-feedback](../usl.md#st-011-collect-post-support-feedback)  
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-13

---

## UI Elements

- **AC-001:** Customers can provide a post-support satisfaction score.
- **AC-002:** Customers can provide a scope clarity score.
- **AC-003:** Customers can optionally provide free-text feedback.

---

## UI Behavior

- **AC-004:** Feedback is requested after support completion or formal handoff.
- **AC-005:** Submitting feedback shows a confirmation to the customer.
- **AC-006:** Customers can skip optional free-text feedback.

---

## Logic

- **AC-007:** Feedback is linked to booking ID, customer ID, and support case ID when available.
- **AC-008:** Feedback can only be submitted once per support case by the same customer contact.

---

## Special Notes

- **AC-009:** Free-text feedback must not be required to submit numeric scores.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **NFR-001:** Feedback submission completes within 2 seconds for 95% of submissions.
- **NFR-002:** Feedback records are available for reporting within 15 minutes for 95% of submissions.
