# ST-001: Acceptance Criteria

**Story:** Evaluate ECC eligibility  
**USL Reference:** [../usl.md#st-001-evaluate-ecc-eligibility](../usl.md#st-001-evaluate-ecc-eligibility)  
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-13

---

## UI Elements

- **AC-001:** The booking flow has an eligibility-controlled ECC service area for export bookings.
- **AC-002:** The ECC service area is hidden when the booking is not eligible.

---

## UI Behavior

- **AC-003:** When a booking is eligible, the customer can proceed to review ECC availability during booking.
- **AC-004:** When eligibility cannot be determined, the booking flow continues without offering ECC.

---

## Logic

- **AC-005:** ECC eligibility requires an export booking, a supported lane, an eligible customer or account, and operations readiness.
- **AC-006:** ECC eligibility fails when compliance, lane, booking, or operations readiness is not approved.

---

## Special Notes

- **AC-007:** The customer must not see unsupported claims such as guaranteed customs clearance during eligibility handling.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **NFR-001:** Eligibility evaluation completes within 500 ms for 95% of booking sessions.
- **NFR-002:** Eligibility decisions are logged with booking ID, customer ID, lane, and decision reason for 99% of evaluated bookings.
