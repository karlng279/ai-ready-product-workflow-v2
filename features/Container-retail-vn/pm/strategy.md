# Product Strategy Canvas

**Product:** Container Retail VN Platform
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Owner:** PM
**Version:** 0.1

---

## Vision

> Every shipping container in Vietnam finds its optimal second life through transparent trade and expert care.

## Mission

> We provide a unified, trusted marketplace and service ecosystem for buying, auctioning, and repairing shipping containers across Vietnam.

---

## Target Customer

**Primary segment:** B2B logistics companies, freight forwarders, and construction firms in Vietnam (SMEs to mid-market). Secondary segment: Individuals/businesses seeking containers for modular housing or temporary storage.

**Customer Job:** "I need to quickly acquire, sell, or repair shipping containers at a fair market price with guaranteed quality so my operations don't stall."

**Current best alternative:** Fragmented local brokers, informal Facebook/Zalo groups, or negotiating directly with shipping lines.

---

## Value Proposition

> For logistics and construction firms in Vietnam who need reliable, cost-effective shipping containers, Container Retail VN is an integrated marketplace that provides verified retail, transparent auctions, and certified repair services. Unlike informal brokers and social media groups, our product guarantees container quality and standardizes pricing.

**Top 3 customer gains this product creates:**
1. Guaranteed quality and condition transparency via standardized inspection reports.
2. Fair market pricing discovery through an open auction system.
3. One-stop shop for post-purchase needs (certified repair and modification).

**Top 3 pains this product relieves:**
1. Risk of getting scammed or buying damaged containers from unverified sellers.
2. The massive time sink of haggling with multiple disconnected brokers.
3. Lack of reliable, standardized repair services to extend container lifespan.

---

## Strategic Position

**Where we play:** The secondary shipping container market in Vietnam (retail, wholesale, repair).

**How we win:** Trust and standardization. By combining marketplace dynamics (retail/auction) with operational excellence (repair network), we become the single source of truth.

**Competitive moat:** Network effects (buyers and sellers converging on one platform) combined with physical operational integration (our certified repair partners).

## Strategic Bets

| Bet | Assumption Behind It | Evidence | What Would Prove It Wrong |
|-----|---------------------|----------|--------------------------|
| Auction Model | Buyers want dynamic pricing rather than just fixed retail prices. | High price variance in informal broker markets. | Auctions fail to clear or take too long, and users default to fixed-price retail. |
| Repair Integration | Offering repair as a service increases trust in the marketplace. | Buyers currently struggle to find reliable post-purchase maintenance. | Buyers prefer to handle repairs entirely in-house or with their own vendors. |
| B2B Focus First | SMEs have higher urgency and LTV than individual buyers. | Construction projects require multiple units at once. | The sales cycle for SMEs is too long compared to B2C modular housing buyers. |

**Strategic non-bets (what we are explicitly NOT doing):**
- International shipping/freight forwarding — we are strictly a domestic container asset marketplace.
- Manufacturing new containers — we focus exclusively on the secondary (used/modified) market.
